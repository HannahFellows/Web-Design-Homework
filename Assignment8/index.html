Material Girl - Madonna

Music Video

Description

"Material Girl" is a music video by Madonna, released in 1985. The video features Madonna in iconic outfits and settings, embodying the materialistic and glamorous culture of the 1980s. The song is known for its catchy chorus and satirical take on materialism. Madonna's performance in the video contributed to her status as a pop culture icon. Wikipedia
